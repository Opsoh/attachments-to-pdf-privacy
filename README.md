# attachments-to-pdf-privacy
Privacy policy for the “Attachments to PDF” iOS app

Attachments to PDF respects your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Collection
Attachments to PDF do not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or identifiers.

Files and Documents
All files you select (such as PDFs and images) are processed locally on your device.
Attachments to PDF do not upload, store, or transmit your files to any server.

Storage
The generated PDF is created on your device and shared using the standard iOS share sheet, allowing you to choose where to save or send it (for example, Files, email, AirDrop, or other apps).
Attachments to PDF do not retain copies of your files.

Third-Party Services
Attachments to PDF do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Permissions
Attachments to PDF only access files that you explicitly select using the iOS document picker. No background or automatic file access occurs.

Children’s Privacy
Attachments to PDF do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
Changes to This Policy
This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
